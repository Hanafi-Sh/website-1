## Judgment (after retry)

**Strengths:**
- Lavender + rose combo is unique in the Indonesian beauty salon market — most competitors default to pink/gold; this stands out.
- Ethereal mood signals "premium experience" — could justify higher price perception.
- Soap bubbles + fairy lights = Instagram-native visual language (TikTok/Reels-friendly).
- Cool purple shadows add depth and modernity.
- Strong emotional pull — viewers feel the dreaminess.

**Weaknesses:**
- Lavender + dreamy vibe doesn't match PRD's "warm" voice — it's cool, not warm.
- The model looks young (early 20s) — PRD targets women 18-45; the older end may feel excluded.
- The composition (woman looking at reflection) is overused in beauty/wellness branding — harder to differentiate.
- Requires very specific supporting UI (glassmorphism, soft purple gradients) to extend the mood — harder to implement cleanly.
- Doesn't naturally highlight the brand name "Daisy" (no daisy flower visible, no salon namesake cue).
- The image can feel "stock-y" if not paired with intentional supporting content.

**Fit with PRD signals:**
- "warm" brand voice: NO — lavender is cool
- "golden hour lighting": NO — twilight lavender
- "natural, candid photo style": PARTIAL — pretty but posed
- "women-owned, personal touch": WEAK — no human warmth visible
- "host hospitality signals": PARTIAL — fairy lights suggest ambiance

**Verdict:** Beautiful image, but pulls against the PRD's "warm hospitality" voice. The lavender palette would require extending a cool tone across the entire site, which conflicts with the PRD's normative rose/gold warm palette.
