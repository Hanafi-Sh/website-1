## Judgment

**Strengths:**
- Captures the "warm hospitality" brand voice perfectly — reviews consistently praise keramahan, hospitality, and "super comfy" vibes; this image IS that vibe.
- The candid, behind-the-scenes feel signals authenticity vs. polished stock-photo territory (PRD explicitly says "avoid stocks that are too polished").
- Subject (stylist) is smiling softly mid-action — communicates warmth without being cheesy.
- Golden hour light through sheer curtains = visual signature of the brand.
- Composition has a clear focal point (stylist's face) with the client in foreground bokeh — professional editorial feel.
- No model agency vibes; looks like real salon photography.

**Weaknesses:**
- Minor artifact: small black speaker cabinet on left has unreadable white text fragment (~3-4 characters, looks like "ST" or logo) — too small to read, but visible if you look. Could be cropped or blurred in post.
- The image skews more "warm beige/terracotta" than "dusty rose" — the PRD's `#C44569` primary isn't strongly present. Brand color needs to come from UI chrome (headings, CTA buttons) rather than this hero image.
- Client (foreground subject) is in partial silhouette — could feel less "centered" than some users expect.

**Fit with PRD signals:**
- "warm" brand voice: PERFECT match
- "golden hour lighting": PERFECT match
- "natural, candid photo style": PERFECT match
- "women-owned, personal touch": Stylist's warm smile reinforces this
- "host hospitality signals (drinks/snacks)": Pampas, jasmine tea, speaker visible

**Verdict:** Strong concept. Slight palette drift from PRD rose toward terracotta, but easily correctable via UI tokens (primary #C44569 stays in buttons/headings). Speaker text artifact is minor.
